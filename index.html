Powerlifting Reading Repo

A repository containing digitised documents from the IPF and original guides from the Yorkshire and North East Powerlifting Federdation.

Available documents

A list of digitised documents created to help new lifters and transcribed from IPF PDFs, created with accessibility and inclusivity in mind.

All IPF documents are unofficial. Documents are created and maintained by a third party.

Last updated 08.11.2023

  • Volunteer Handbook

  • IPF Technical Rules

    The official 2024 rules of powerlifting in the IPF.

  • IPF Technical Rules (Plain English)

    An easier to read version of the 2024 IPF rules.

  • IPF Approved Kit List

    A list of clothing and equipment approved for 2024.

  • YNEPF Competition Calendar
